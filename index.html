URANIUM-235

History of Uranium-235

Uranium-235, a naturally occuring radioactive isotope, holds significance in power generation and weapon development. Its fissile nature, the ability to undergo nuclear fission upon absorbing a slow neutron, makes is crucial for these applications.

The discovery of Uranium-235 in 1935 paved its way for its utilization in the Manhattan Project during World War II. Scientist within the project recognized its potential as a fissile material for nuclear weapons.
